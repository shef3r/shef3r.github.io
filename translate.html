translate

Translate is a Google Translate client that allows you to quickly and easily translate text from one language to another. designed for Windows 10/11 devices, this UWP app is optimized for running much smoother than the official web app :3
with Translate, you can easily input text in one language and receive a (optionally real-time) translation in another language. the app is powered by Google Translate, ensuring that your translations are accurate and up-to-date.
whether you want to quickly check a word's translation or you're communicating with international clients/friends, Translate makes it easy to overcome language barriers. you can also customize the font and its size to make your translations easier to read.
in addition to the Windows 10/11 version, there are also versions for Windows Phone 8.1/1 and an Android version possibly coming.
